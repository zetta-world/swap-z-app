## Outline — 5 scenes

**Scene 1 — Establishing shot: architecture in 10 seconds** (~0:10)
*On screen:* Landing page (hero + swap card visible), quick cut to `/about` — architecture
layer diagram held for 3 seconds. Static lower-third appears: **"11 live pages · 13 chains ·
real integrations — no mocks."** No voiceover needed; title card only.
*Purpose:* lets the grant-committee reviewer see technical substance in the first 10 s before
the product demo begins.

**Scene 2 — Live swap: route aggregation in action** (~1:00)
*On screen:* Navigate to `/` → select ETH → USDC on Arbitrum. Pause on quote panel showing
route split (0x v2 primary, LiFi fallback label visible). Click "Swap" through wallet confirm
modal — show non-custodial language ("Your keys, your funds"). Transaction pending → confirmed.
Lower-third badge: **"0x v2 · LiFi · CoW Protocol"**.
*Purpose:* proves real on-chain execution, not a demo wallet.

**Scene 3 — ZION AI pair analysis** (~1:00)
*On screen:* Open `/pro` terminal → search a mid-cap Solana token. ZION dock expands —
conviction badge animates in (BULLISH / NEUTRAL / BEARISH). Scroll down: GoPlus risk score +
Honeypot.is flag visible. AI reasoning text streams (Claude Haiku 4.5 output). Cut to
GeckoTerminal chart in the same view.
Lower-third badge: **"Anthropic Claude Haiku 4.5 · GoPlus · Honeypot.is · GeckoTerminal"**.
*Purpose:* shows AI layer is live, not marketing copy.

**Scene 4 — CEX autopilot rebalance** (~0:45)
*On screen:* Navigate to `/cex` → show real CEX balance rollup (CCXT, read-only API keys,
non-custodial label explicitly visible in UI). Trigger autopilot rebalance — show the order
preview panel + confirmation. Cut to `/orders` — new order entry appears in history.
Lower-third badge: **"CCXT · 10+ CEX · non-custodial read-only keys"**.
*Purpose:* demonstrates the CEX-bridge differentiator and reinforces non-custodial posture.

**Scene 5 — Multi-chain portfolio + closing thesis** (~0:55)
*On screen:* `/portfolio` — multi-chain token breakdown visible (ETH, SOL, ARB chains).
Slow scroll. Cut to black title card:
> **"Non-custodial · Multi-chain · AI-overlayed"**
> *"Applying to Solana Foundation Grant (developer tooling + DeFi infra) &*
> *submitting to Colosseum Eternal hackathon."*
> `swap-z.app` — final frame 5 seconds.
*Purpose:* closes with grant context so reviewers know immediately why they're watching.
